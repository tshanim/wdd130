Our Approach, Services, and Benefits

Most of us are familiar with the image of the traditional massage table and oils. But massage doesn't fall into just one category. There are many different types, and each one achieves different results. Toucher Wellness Therapy uses different types of massage and methods, called modalities, to target specific problems or areas for customized results. We understand that not all modalities are suitable for everyone, and that you may find a type of massage that suits your personal needs and philosophy.

In addition, we place even greater emphasis on the therapeutic benefits of massage by using the right oils to achieve the desired massage effect. Our therapists will consult you first to find out which types of essential oils you prefer. Our clients benefit from regular participation in our therapy: renewed vitality, reduced stress levels, increased productivity, improved work performance, enhanced self-esteem and much more.

Stress is inevitable. Too much can kill

Stress is perhaps the most common health problem facing adults and young people today. One of the factors contributing to stress is the amount of harmful additives present in the foods we consume on a daily basis. Research has shown that up to 2 kg of harmful additives a year are ingested into our bodies, resulting in degenerative conditions. Whether it is the stress of office work, the stress of personal relationships, etc., it is omnipresent and can lead to potentially dangerous health problems.

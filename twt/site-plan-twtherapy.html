Overview

Purpose

Toucher Wellness Therapy offers stress relief and stress management services to help people live healthier, more productive lives. Our services are comprehensive and cover the full spectrum of personal health and body therapy. TWT focuses on relieving deep muscle tissue, pain and discomfort using new and innovative techniques and non-harmful, non-additive products. We also offer free advice sessions on what to do and how to improve the quality of life in a holistic way.

Audience

Target audience:

  • People who are physically fit and want to lead a healthy lifestyle
  • People recovering from injury or accident or suffering from chronic pain
  • People who want to pamper themselves
  • People who prefer alternative health care
  • Athletes (footballers, cyclists, gymnasts, dancers, etc.)
  • Stressed-out people who need relaxation (e.g., high-stress executives, etc.)
  • Elderly people with physical problems
  • Pregnant women
  • Weekend warriors
  • People following self-improvement programs (e.g., addiction recovery programs)
  • People undergoing pre- and post-operative recovery
  • People with disabilities
  • The entertainment industry
  • Students

Content

Home page

Stress is inevitable. Too much can kill

Stress is perhaps the most common health problem facing adults and young people today. Whether it's the stress of office work, personal relationships, a sick or lost family member, job security, finances, impending assignment, or social stress, it's omnipresent. It can lead to potentially dangerous health problems, alcohol and drug abuse, relationship problems, anxiety, risk of self-harm and deterioration of overall well-being.

Massage Therapy for Body, Mind, and Soul

Most people know what it is like to be stressed out. However, it is not always easy to understand what stress is and how it manifests itself in our lives. Toucher Wellness Therapy strives to help you recognize signs of stress through physical and emotional assessment. Thanks to the expertise of our qualified staff, we offer you the opportunity to treat stress in a therapeutic and natural way.

Services

Our Approach, Services, and Benefits

Most of us are familiar with the image of the traditional massage table and oils. But massage doesn't fall into just one category. There are many different types, and each one achieves different results. Toucher Wellness Therapy uses different types of massage and methods, called modalities, to target specific problems or areas for customized results. We understand that not all modalities are suitable for everyone, and that you may find a type of massage that suits your personal needs and philosophy.

In addition, we place even greater emphasis on the therapeutic benefits of massage by using the right oils to achieve the desired massage effect. Our therapists will consult you first to find out which types of essential oils you prefer. Our clients benefit from regular participation in our therapy: renewed vitality, reduced stress levels, increased productivity, improved work performance, enhanced self-esteem and much more.

Mind. Body. Soul

Everyone wants to be treated well, loved, and touched. Toucher Wellness Therapy is here to care for you, to help you heal, body, mind and soul, and to ease all pain and negative energy. Something humankind wants to feel : a new sense of relief and nursing, comfort, and wellness. We can help you reach a higher aspect of yourself.

About Us

Toucher Wellness Therapy (TWT)

Toucher Wellness Therapy was founded with the aim of helping people experiencing pain or any form of stress to find relief through message therapy. Massage therapy accelerates the healing process, improves circulation, and can even restore emotional balance. It makes people feel good, happy, and confident. We believe that a healthier body also contributes to a better way of life, adding quality to an individual's existence and faculties. We are committed to treating the whole person with the sole purpose of improving their well-being.

Our Philosophy and Added Values

TWT's philosophy is to integrate massage therapy and relaxation to connect body, mind and soul. This philosophy and the understanding of our service are rooted in the intention and thinking of our therapists: healing and mindfulness with your well-being. During our therapy sessions, we promote a sense of being present with you and working with you, rather than on you. We take great pride in our service and our ability to help you heal and relax.

As a guiding principle of adding value to the overall wellness of our customers, TWT provides its services in a nurturing, educational and eco-friendly environment. We offer natural choices and a wide range of products, all illustrated by the finest aesthetic and massage therapies. We believe that wellness is a process, not a procedure. We use proven traditional techniques and naturally enhance them by incorporating natural products and beauty innovations. This allows us to create an impeccable massage therapy experience.

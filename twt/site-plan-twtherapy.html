Overview

Purpose

Toucher Wellness Therapy offers stress relief and stress management services to help people live healthier, more productive lives. Our services are comprehensive and cover the full spectrum of personal health and body therapy. TWT focuses on relieving deep muscle tissue, pain and discomfort using new and innovative techniques and non-harmful, non-additive products. We also offer free advice sessions on what to do and how to improve the quality of life in a holistic way.

Audience

Target audience:

  • People who are physically fit and want to lead a healthy lifestyle
  • People recovering from injury or accident or suffering from chronic pain
  • People who want to pamper themselves
  • People who prefer alternative health care
  • Athletes (footballers, cyclists, gymnasts, dancers, etc.)
  • Stressed-out people who need relaxation (e.g., high-stress executives, etc.)
  • Elderly people with physical problems
  • Pregnant women
  • Weekend warriors
  • People following self-improvement programs (e.g., addiction recovery programs)
  • People undergoing pre- and post-operative recovery
  • People with disabilities
  • The entertainment industry
  • Students

Home page

Stress is inevitable. Too much can kill

Stress is perhaps the most common health problem facing adults and young people today. Whether it's the stress of office work, the stress of personal relationships, the stress of a sick family member or loss of a family member, the stress of job security, financial stress, social stress, or the stress of impending assignment, it's omnipresent and can lead to potentially dangerous health problems, alcohol and drug dependency, relationship problems, anxiety, risk of self-harm and general deterioration of overall well-being.

Although you may know what it's like to be stressed out, understanding what stress is and how it manifests itself in your life isn't always an easy task for most people. As a provider of stress relief and stress management services, Toucher Wellness Therapy strives to help its clients recognize the visible and invisible symptoms of stress with the help of state-of-the-art technology, and offers you the opportunity to treat stress therapeutically and naturally thanks to the expertise of its qualified staff, who have been offering therapeutic and stress management services for many years and have helped bring relief and a healthier lifestyle to many people.

Another factor contributing to stress is the amount of harmful additives present in the food we consume on a daily basis. Research has shown that up to 2 kg of harmful additives per year are ingested into our bodies, contributing to degenerative conditions. Toucher Wellness Therapy has an exhaustive A to Z guide to herbal remedies and natural products useful for purging the body. The African natural wrap method for foot and hand massage is one example of our innovative techniques. In addition to physical and emotional assessment and services, we can also provide an analysis of your nutritional needs. We work closely with nutritionists and other accredited organizations in the field of phytotherapy and natural therapies.

Services

Our approach, services, and benefits

Most of us are familiar with the image of a traditional massage table and oils. But massage doesn't fall into just one category. There are many different types of massage, and each one achieves different results. Toucher Wellness Therapy practices different types of massage and methods called modalities to target specific problems or areas of concern for customized results. At Toucher Wellness Therapy, we understand that not all modalities are suitable for everyone, and that you may find a type of massage that suits your personal needs and philosophy.

In addition, we place even greater emphasis on the therapeutic benefits of massage by using the right oils to reduce friction between hands and skin, for that delicious gliding sensation, to moisturize the skin and to achieve the desired massage effect. Our therapists will consult you first to find out which types of essential oils you prefer.

Our clients who regularly participate in our stress management program experience renewed vitality, healthy sleep, restored deep breathing, relief from respiratory ailments, reduced stress levels, prevention and relief of headaches, improved blood circulation and lymphatic drainage, enhanced productivity and concentration, improved work performance, development of leadership skills, improved self-esteem, enhanced mental health, positive effect on relationships.

About Us

Our added values

Toucher Wellness Therapy was founded with the idea of helping people experiencing pain, recovering from injury, or suffering from stress and other addictions to find relief through message therapy. Massage therapy accelerates the healing process, improves circulation and can even restore emotional balance. Massage makes people feel good, happy and confident. We believe that a healthier body also contributes to a better way of life, adding quality to an individual's existence and faculties. We are committed to treating the whole person with a single eye on enhancing the sense of well-being.

Toucher Wellness Therapy is guided by the following values: to provide a nurturing, educational and eco-friendly environment, to offer natural choices and a wide range of products, all illustrated by the very best aesthetic and massage services. We believe that wellness is a process, not a procedure. We use proven traditional techniques and naturally enhance them by incorporating natural products and beauty innovations. This allows us to create an impeccable massage therapy experience.

As a result, we are able to offer comprehensive stress management services that cover the entire spectrum of personal health and body therapy. These include aromatherapy, Swedish massage, reflexology, scalp massage, back, neck and shoulder massage, as well as chiropractic physiotherapy. Our staff are trained in these fields and have been providing therapeutic services for many years. They have helped to bring relief and a healthier lifestyle to many dedicated clients throughout the region.

Toucher Wellness Therapy (TWT)

Toucher Wellness Therapy was founded with the aim of helping people experiencing pain or any form of stress to find relief through message therapy. Massage therapy accelerates the healing process, improves circulation, and can even restore emotional balance. It makes people feel good, happy, and confident. We believe that a healthier body also contributes to a better way of life, adding quality to an individual's existence and faculties. We are committed to treating the whole person with the sole purpose of improving their well-being.

Our Philosophy and Added Values

TWT's philosophy is to integrate massage therapy and relaxation to connect body, mind and soul. This philosophy and the understanding of our service are rooted in the intention and thinking of our therapists: healing and mindfulness with your well-being. During our therapy sessions, we promote a sense of being present with you and working with you, rather than on you. We take great pride in our service and our ability to help you heal and relax.

As a guiding principle of adding value to the overall wellness of our customers, TWT provides its services in a nurturing, educational and eco-friendly environment. We offer natural choices and a wide range of products, all illustrated by the finest aesthetic and massage therapies. We believe that wellness is a process, not a procedure. We use proven traditional techniques and naturally enhance them by incorporating natural products and beauty innovations. This allows us to create an impeccable massage therapy experience.
